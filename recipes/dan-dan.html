Dan Dan Noodles

Description

Sweet, sour, salty, umami and mala from Sichuan pepper, dan dan noodles will send your taste buds to flavor town!

The best part is that this dish is pantry friednly and can me made in the amount of time that it takes to cook a serving of pasta. The most difficult part of this dish may be sourcing a few uncommon ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 125 grams noodles
  • 2 teaspoons black vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon tahini
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons chili oil
  • ground Sichuan pepper, to taste
  • chopped cilantro, scallion and roasted peanuts for garnish

Preparation

  1. Bring to a boil just enough water to cover the noodles and then add them to the cooking vessel.
  2. While the noodles cook, mix the non-garnish ingredients in a serving bowl large enough to toss the noodles.
  3. Once the noodles are cooked through, add them to the serving bowl along with a couple of tablespoons of the cooking water from the noodles. This water will help to thicken the sauce.
  4. Toss the noodles with the sauce until it thickens to your liking, adding more water from the cooking vessel as desired.
  5. Garnish, drool and consume!
